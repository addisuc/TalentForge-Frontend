## Getting Started

### What is TalentForge?

TalentForge connects job seekers with opportunities. You can:
- Create a professional profile
- Search and apply for jobs
- Track application status
- Manage interviews
- Communicate with recruiters

---

## Creating Your Account

### Registration

1. Click **"Sign Up"**
2. Enter:
   - Email address
   - Password (8+ characters, uppercase, lowercase, number, special character)
   - First and last name
   - Phone number
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Check email for verification link
5. Click link to verify
6. Log in with credentials

---

## Building Your Profile

### Basic Information

1. Go to **"My Profile"**
2. Add:
   - Professional title
   - Location
   - Phone number
   - LinkedIn profile
   - Portfolio/website

### Work Experience

1. Click **"Add Experience"**
2. Enter:
   - Job title
   - Company name
   - Start/end dates
   - Description
3. Click **"Save"**

### Education

1. Click **"Add Education"**
2. Enter:
   - Degree
   - Field of study
   - Institution
   - Graduation year
3. Click **"Save"**

### Skills

1. Click **"Add Skill"**
2. Enter skill name
3. Select proficiency level
4. Click **"Add"**

### Resume Upload

1. Click **"Upload Resume"**
2. Select file (PDF, DOC, DOCX - Max 5MB)
3. Click **"Upload"**

## Searching for Jobs

### Browse Jobs

1. Click **"Browse Jobs"**
2. View all available positions

### Use Filters

- **Keywords**: Job title, skills
- **Location**: City, state, remote
- **Job Type**: Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Internship
- **Experience Level**: Entry, Mid, Senior, Executive
- **Salary Range**: Min/max
- **Posted Date**: 24 hours, 7 days, 30 days

### View Job Details

1. Click job card
2. Review:
   - Description
   - Requirements
   - Salary
   - Benefits
   - Company info

### Save Jobs

1. Click **"Save"** icon
2. Access from **"Saved Jobs"**

## Applying for Jobs

### Application Steps

1. Click **"Apply Now"**
2. Review pre-filled information
3. Add cover letter (optional)
4. Answer screening questions
5. Click **"Submit Application"**
6. Receive confirmation

### Tips

✅ Tailor cover letter  
✅ Update resume  
✅ Apply to matching jobs  
✅ Follow up after 2 weeks

---

## Tracking Applications

### Application Dashboard

1. Go to **"My Applications"**
2. View all applications
3. Check status

### Application Statuses

- **Applied** - Submitted
- **Under Review** - Being reviewed
- **Shortlisted** - Selected for next round
- **Interview Scheduled** - Interview set
- **Interview Completed** - Awaiting decision
- **Offer Extended** - Job offer made
- **Hired** - Offer accepted
- **Rejected** - Not successful
- **Withdrawn** - You withdrew

### Withdraw Application

1. Open application
2. Click **"Withdraw"**
3. Confirm

## Interview Management

### View Interviews

1. Go to **"My Interviews"**
2. View:
   - Date/time
   - Type (Phone, Video, In-person)
   - Duration
   - Interviewer
   - Meeting link
   - Instructions

### Join Video Interview

1. Click **"Join Interview"** (10 min before)
2. Test camera/microphone
3. Wait in lobby

### Reschedule

1. Open interview
2. Click **"Request Reschedule"**
3. Provide reason
4. Suggest alternatives
5. Wait for confirmation

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

1. Click **"Forgot Password"**
2. Enter email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password

### Application Not Submitting

1. Check required fields
2. Ensure resume under 5MB
3. Try different browser
4. Clear cache

### Not Receiving Notifications

1. Check spam folder
2. Verify email in settings
3. Check notification preferences
4. Add noreply@talentforge.com to contacts
