## Getting Started

### What is the Client Portal?

The Client Portal allows you to:
- Submit job requests
- Review submitted candidates
- Provide feedback
- Schedule interviews
- Track hiring progress
- Communicate with recruiters

### Accessing the Portal

1. Receive invitation email from recruiter
2. Click invitation link
3. Set your password
4. Log in to Client Portal

## Job Requests

### Submit New Job Request

1. Click **"New Job Request"**
2. Enter job details:
   - **Job Title**: Position name
   - **Department**: Team/division
   - **Location**: Office location or remote
   - **Job Type**: Full-time, Part-time, Contract
   - **Experience Level**: Entry, Mid, Senior, Executive
   - **Salary Range**: Budget range
   - **Start Date**: When position needs to be filled
3. Add job description:
   - Role overview
   - Key responsibilities
   - Required qualifications
   - Preferred qualifications
   - Team structure
   - Growth opportunities
4. Specify requirements:
   - Must-have skills
   - Nice-to-have skills
   - Education requirements
   - Certifications needed
5. Set priorities:
   - Urgency level
   - Number of positions
   - Hiring timeline
6. Click **"Submit Request"**

### Track Job Request Status

**Request Statuses:**
- **Submitted**: Request received
- **Under Review**: Recruiter reviewing
- **Approved**: Job posting created
- **Active**: Recruiting in progress
- **On Hold**: Temporarily paused
- **Filled**: Position filled
- **Cancelled**: Request cancelled

### Edit Job Request

1. Go to **"Job Requests"**
2. Click request to edit
3. Click **"Edit"**
4. Update information
5. Click **"Save Changes"**
6. Recruiter notified of changes

### Close Job Request

1. Open job request
2. Click **"Close Request"**
3. Select reason:
   - Position filled
   - No longer needed
   - Budget constraints
   - On hold
4. Provide details
5. Confirm

## Reviewing Candidates

### View Submitted Candidates

1. Go to **"Candidates"**
2. Filter by:
   - Job request
   - Submission date
   - Status
   - Rating
3. Click candidate to review

### Candidate Profile

Review candidate information:
- **Resume**: Download and review
- **Cover Letter**: Read application letter
- **Work Experience**: Employment history
- **Education**: Academic background
- **Skills**: Technical and soft skills
- **Recruiter Notes**: Submission notes

### Rate Candidates

1. Open candidate profile
2. Click **"Rate Candidate"**
3. Select rating:
   - ⭐ Not a fit
   - ⭐⭐ Below expectations
   - ⭐⭐⭐ Meets requirements
   - ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Exceeds expectations
   - ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Exceptional candidate
4. Add comments
5. Click **"Submit Rating"**

### Candidate Actions

**Approve for Interview:**
1. Click **"Approve"**
2. Select interview type
3. Suggest dates/times
4. Click **"Submit"**

**Request More Information:**
1. Click **"Request Info"**
2. Specify what's needed
3. Click **"Send"**

**Reject Candidate:**
1. Click **"Reject"**
2. Select reason
3. Add feedback (optional)
4. Click **"Confirm"**

### Shortlist Candidates

1. Click **"Add to Shortlist"**
2. Candidate added to favorites
3. Access from **"Shortlisted"** tab
4. Compare shortlisted candidates

## Interview Coordination

### Review Interview Requests

1. Go to **"Interviews"**
2. View pending requests
3. Review candidate details
4. Check suggested times

### Schedule Interview

1. Click **"Schedule"** on request
2. Select:
   - Interview type (Phone, Video, In-person)
   - Date and time
   - Duration
   - Interviewers from your team
   - Location or meeting link
3. Add interview instructions
4. Click **"Confirm"**
5. All parties notified

### Reschedule Interview

1. Open interview
2. Click **"Reschedule"**
3. Select new date/time
4. Provide reason
5. Click **"Update"**
6. Notifications sent

### Cancel Interview

1. Open interview
2. Click **"Cancel"**
3. Provide reason
4. Confirm cancellation

### Join Video Interview

1. Click **"Join Interview"** (5 min before)
2. Test camera and microphone
3. Wait for candidate
4. Conduct interview

### Submit Interview Feedback

1. After interview, click **"Add Feedback"**
2. Rate candidate performance:
   - Technical skills
   - Communication
   - Cultural fit
   - Problem-solving
   - Overall impression
3. Enter detailed feedback
4. Recommend decision:
   - Move forward
   - Additional interview
   - Reject
5. Click **"Submit"**

## Feedback & Communication

### Provide Candidate Feedback

**Quick Feedback:**
1. Open candidate profile
2. Click **"Quick Feedback"**
3. Select:
   - Interested
   - Not interested
   - Need more info
4. Add brief comment
5. Click **"Send"**

**Detailed Feedback:**
1. Click **"Detailed Feedback"**
2. Rate on multiple criteria
3. Provide comprehensive comments
4. Suggest next steps
5. Click **"Submit"**

### Message Recruiter

1. Click **"Messages"**
2. Select recruiter or click **"New Message"**
3. Enter subject and message
4. Attach files if needed
5. Click **"Send"**

### Request Updates

1. Open job request
2. Click **"Request Update"**
3. Specify what information needed
4. Click **"Send"**
5. Recruiter responds within 24 hours

### Notifications

Receive notifications for:
- New candidate submissions
- Interview requests
- Candidate status updates
- Recruiter messages
- Job request updates

**Manage Notifications:**
1. Go to **"Settings"** → **"Notifications"**
2. Toggle email/in-app notifications
3. Set notification frequency
4. Click **"Save"**

## Hiring Process

### Make Hiring Decision

1. Review all interview feedback
2. Compare shortlisted candidates
3. Discuss with team
4. Select final candidate

### Request Offer

1. Open candidate profile
2. Click **"Request Offer"**
3. Enter offer details:
   - Position title
   - Salary
   - Start date
   - Benefits
   - Special terms
4. Add notes for recruiter
5. Click **"Submit"**

### Track Offer Status

**Offer Stages:**
- **Requested**: Offer request submitted
- **In Preparation**: Recruiter preparing
- **Pending Approval**: Internal approval
- **Extended**: Offer sent to candidate
- **Accepted**: Candidate accepted
- **Declined**: Candidate declined
- **Negotiating**: In negotiation

### Onboarding Coordination

1. Once offer accepted
2. Coordinate with recruiter on:
   - Start date
   - Onboarding schedule
   - Equipment needs
   - Access requirements
3. Prepare team for new hire

## Best Practices

### Job Requests

✅ Provide detailed requirements  
✅ Set realistic salary ranges  
✅ Define clear success criteria  
✅ Specify must-have vs nice-to-have  
✅ Include team/culture information

### Candidate Review

✅ Review within 48 hours  
✅ Provide specific feedback  
✅ Be consistent in evaluation  
✅ Consider diverse candidates  
✅ Focus on skills and fit

### Interview Process

✅ Prepare questions in advance  
✅ Be punctual  
✅ Take detailed notes  
✅ Assess objectively  
✅ Provide timely feedback

### Communication

✅ Respond to recruiter within 24 hours  
✅ Be clear and specific  
✅ Provide constructive feedback  
✅ Keep recruiter informed of changes  
✅ Maintain professional tone

## Account Settings

### Update Profile

1. Go to **"Settings"** → **"Profile"**
2. Update:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Job title
   - Department
3. Click **"Save"**

### Change Password

1. Go to **"Settings"** → **"Security"**
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password
5. Confirm new password
6. Click **"Update"**

### Manage Team Access

1. Go to **"Settings"** → **"Team"**
2. View team members with access
3. Add new members:
   - Click **"Invite Member"**
   - Enter email
   - Set permissions
   - Click **"Send Invite"**
4. Remove access if needed

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How quickly will I see candidates?**  
A: Typically within 3-5 business days of job request approval.

**Q: Can I interview candidates directly?**  
A: Yes, coordinate through the portal with your recruiter.

**Q: What if I need to change job requirements?**  
A: Edit the job request or message your recruiter.

**Q: How do I provide feedback?**  
A: Use the feedback forms in each candidate profile.

**Q: Can multiple team members access the portal?**  
A: Yes, invite team members through Settings.

**Q: What if a candidate declines our offer?**  
A: Recruiter will present additional candidates.
